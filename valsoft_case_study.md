# Case Study: AI Opportunities at DockMaster

## Situation 
As part of your role at Valsoft as the AI Venture Builder, you’ve been assigned to
evaluate one of our portfolio companies to identify opportunities for AI-driven product
development and commercialization. For this case, your focus is [DockMaster](https://www.dockmaster.com/), a business
operating in the maritime software sector.

## Company Overview 
For decades maritime management teams used spreadsheets, paper
logs, and disconnected tools that slowed workflows, caused delays, and created costly
mistakes that reduced both time and revenue. DockMaster gives modern marine
businesses a digital solution that helps them operate faster, more accurately, and with less
effort. DockMaster replaces manual processes with marine digital tools, organizing and
centralizing data, automating repetitive tasks, and providing managers with real-time
insights that improve decisions across every aspect of operations. For hundreds of
marinas, DockMaster is the primary system of record for financial and operational
functions including sales, inventory, accounting, maintenance and services.

## Stakeholders 
1. Dockmaster Customers
&nbsp; - Marina Operators
&nbsp; - Boatyards
&nbsp; - Marine Service Providers
&nbsp; - Yacht Clubs
&nbsp; - Boat Rental Businesses
2. Boatyard and Marina Staff

## Deliverable
Your task is to identify opportunities where DockMaster could develop meaningful,
revenue-driving AI features or standalone products. Focus on the areas you believe matter
most and explain the reasoning behind your choices. Finally, you will build a prototype of
the product and outline a clear Go-To-Market strategy detailing how the product will be
positioned, sold, and scaled to customers.
